ABOUT


StickerMe! creatively visualizes personality traits
and frames the user with the cute characters.




HOW TO


(1) Tap on the camera frame
(2) Press the keys to create a custom personality frame
(3) Click the pink button to save your picture
(4) Click the green button to undo
(5) Click the blue button to restart



Describe yourself,
spell your name or a word,
or describe your friends!
